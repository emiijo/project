Selectors

SELECTORS and their syntax:



BASIC SELECTORS: To identify what specifically you wish to style.

  • elementname
  • .classname
  • #idname
  • [attr=value]
  • *


COMBINATORS: To style several selectors at the same time.

  • selectorA + selectorB = Adjacent sibling
  • selectorA ~ selectorB = General sibling
  • parent > child = Direct child
  • parent decendent = Decendent


PSUEDO SELECTORS: Add style to specific actions/events.

  • :active
  • :hover
  • :visited
  • focus


One can visit W3 School for more information regarding selectors in CSS.
